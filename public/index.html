PomodoroTime

A simple time-tracking app to help people manage their time and energy more effectively.

How does it work?

Time is broken down into two periods: work and breaks. The timer will first begin a work period for the set amount of time. During this time, you work on a single task without distractions. When time is up, you rate your session experience and start a break. This method encourages breaking down larger tasks into smaller ones that could be completed with a small timeframe such as 25 minutes.
